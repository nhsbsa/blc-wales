The baby loss certificate is an official document issued on behalf of the Secretary of State for Health and Social Care. It will be printed on watermarked paper.

The wording on the certificate is as follows:

Certificate of baby loss under 24 weeks gestation.

Issued on behalf of the Secretary of State for Health and Social Care.

  • Certificate reference
  • Date of issue
  • Name and surname of parent
  • Name and surname of any other parent
  • Name of baby (optional)
  • Sex (if known)
  • Date of loss (if known)
  • Place of loss (if known)

Information: This certificate is produced following a self-declaration by the parent.

This certificate cannot be used as evidence of identity and does not allow any entitlement to any services or benefits.

Certificate of baby loss under 24 weeks gestation.
